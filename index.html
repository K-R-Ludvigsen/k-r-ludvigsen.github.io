Kaspar Rosager Ludvigsen, Law/Cybersecurity PhD Student

Danish lawyer (jurist), PhD is the intersection between law and cybersecurity + themes that surround IoT/CPS/HCI at the University of Strathclyde in Scotland. Yes, I can read math due to my weird background on top of being a lawyer, and yes, I too dislike that law has not adopted formal logic yet.

My advisors are Reader Shishir Nagaraja and Professor Angela Daly.

Affiliations are the Department of Computer & Information Sciences, Centre for Internet Law and Policy and Strathcyber.

I am vividly interested and proficient in any kind of law, current working areas are many, some would be European Law, "Cyberlaw", medical device regulation, public law and torts and their equivalents.

In cybersecurity, I am both very intrigued and interested in the general methodology and understanding of it, as well as the epistemology of attacking and defending, side-channel attacks and social engineering. Like above, it is really everything, but see my publications for my current obsessions.
